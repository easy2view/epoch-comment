 <p>
  【大纪元4月12日讯】（一）如何发表
  <ok href="https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/tag/%E4%B8%89%E9%80%80%E5%A3%B0%E6%98%8E.html">
   三退声明
  </ok>
 </p>
 <p>
  1、突破网络封锁进入动态网、无界网络或花园网站首页(大陆以外读者不需此步)
 </p>
 <p>
  2、（以进入动态网首页为例，看图）查找大纪元点击，进入首页
 </p>
 <p>
  <center>
   <br/>
   <ok href=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/04/5041246331695-450x425.jpg" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">

   </ok>
  </center>
 </p>
 <p>
  3、图示为大纪元首页
 </p>
 <p>
  4、点击退党声明浏览
 </p>
 <p>
  <center>
   <br/>
   <ok href=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/04/5041245381695-450x347.jpg" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
    <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-7428938" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/04/5041245381695-450x347.jpg" title=""/>
   </ok>
  </center>
 </p>
 <p>
  5、向下查找直到出现图示
 </p>
 <p>
  <center>
   <br/>
   <ok href=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/04/5041243051695-450x425.jpg" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
    <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-7428939" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/04/5041243051695-450x425.jpg" title=""/>
   </ok>
  </center>
 </p>
 <p>
  需填写的地方
  <br/>
  （1）姓 名：___________
  <br/>
  （2）来 自：___________
  <br/>
  （3）标 题：____________________
  <br/>
  （4）人 数：_____
  <br/>
  （5）内 容：
  <br/>
  ___________________________
  <br/>
  ___________________________
 </p>
 <p>
  6、校对完毕后点击发表
 </p>
 <p>
  7、若出现
 </p>
 <p>
  <center>
   <br/>
   <ok href=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/04/5041240561695-450x413.jpg" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
    <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-7428941" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/04/5041240561695-450x413.jpg" title=""/>
   </ok>
  </center>
 </p>
 <p>
  3、键入 关键词
 </p>
 <p>
  4、选择 名字、标题、正文或来自其中之一
 </p>
 <p>
  5、点击 搜索
 </p>
 <p>
  6、带有关键词的内容就会出现
